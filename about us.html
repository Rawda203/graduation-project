About CipherSpire

CipherSpire is a creator of cybersecurity
technologies to investigate, prevent,
and fight digital crime.

Global Fight Against Cybercrime

At CipherSpire, our dedicated team of over 400 cybersecurity experts works around the clock—24/7/365—to proactively defend organizations against both global and localized cyber threats. We ensure uninterrupted operations while staying ahead of evolving digital risks.

Unified Risk Platform

CipherSpire’s Unified Risk Platform is an ecosystem of intelligence-driven solutions that offer the widest cyber threat coverage, map each organization’s threat profile, and tailor defenses in real-time from a single interface.

Incident Response & Investigations

Our incident response and investigations are industry-leading with 1550+ successful investigations across 60+ countries and holding the #1 position for incident response retainers.

Digital Crime Resistance Centers (DCRCs)

Our mission-critical Digital Crime Resistance Centers (DCRC) located in 7 countries of Asia, Europe, Middle East, Africa, and Central Asia help CipherSpire strengthen its contribution to global cybercrime prevention and continually expand threat-hunting capabilities.
